Who I am

Wired for problems that don't come with instructions.

I'm a civil engineer with two and a half years in geotechnical consulting, specifically tailings engineering, delivering projects across South Africa, Ghana, and Angola. I ran the full project lifecycle: proposals, design, modelling, on site QA, reporting, and direct client work.

I'm drawn to the intersection of engineering, data, and decision making. I move fast, back my own judgement, and care deeply about the quality of what I put my name on. At SRK, I was lead author on reports for clients, contributed to a published paper on seepage modelling, and pushed the office to adopt AI tools through testing, internal presentations, and peer training.

Between SRK and now I took a short personal chapter, in the same spirit as my gap year, to back myself, step outside my lane, and learn something new. Back in Cape Town now, ready to apply my analytical foundation in a broader business, strategic, or operator role.
